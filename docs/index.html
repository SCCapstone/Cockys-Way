Why Use Cocky's Way?

Cocky's Way was created to address a challenge faced by nearly every student at the University of South Carolina: finding your way around campus shouldn't be stressful. Whether it's your first semester or your final year, navigating a sprawling and often confusing campus can be frustrating, especially when you're running late for class or trying to locate a professor’s office. Cocky's Way is designed to remove that barrier by providing intuitive, step-by-step guidance tailored specifically to USC's layout. The app gives you fast and reliable directions, helps you discover shortcuts you didn’t know existed, and ensures that you always know exactly where you’re going — no paper maps, no guesswork, and no wasted time.

In addition to our superb navigation features, Cocky’s Way also provides users, with tools to stay organized and on track throughout the semester. The built-in schedule manager lets you store your courses all in one place, so you can easily see where you need to be and when, along with any course information you may need. To make sure you’re always prepared, the app also sends timely notifications when a class is about to begin so you never arrive late. You can even connect your profile to your BlackBoard calendar to easily view your assignments and deadlines. Cocky’s Way is designed to be your all-in-one solution for navigating campus and managing your academic life.

How to Use Cocky's Way

Cocky's Way is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive. To get started, simply download the app from the link above. Once installed, you can open the app and will be greeted by the map and a tutorial. The tutorial is very useful if this is your first time using the app, as it walks you through all of the features of our map screen. After completing the tutorial, you can begin to explore the different locations around campus. Make sure to check out the "Filter Pins" feature to add or remove markers from the map to find what you want. Using the profile icon on the top right of each screen, you can create a profile or login. When selecting a marker a popup will appear, displaying all of the marker's information, including all the buttons and features available to you. You can start navigation to the location (from your current location), set the marker as your start location for future directions to other markers, and even save the location to your favorites for easy access later.

Each of the tabs at the bottom of the screen correspond to a set of pages to customize your experience and explore the UofSC.

  • Directory: Search for professors, view office hours and contact info.
  • Schedule: Add and track your classes, set notifications, view a calendar with assignments and courses.
  • Settings: Change the theme, manage data, and link your Blackboard calendar.

Cocky's Way is designed to be your all-in-one solution for navigating campus and managing your academic life. We hope you enjoy using it as much as we enjoyed creating it!
